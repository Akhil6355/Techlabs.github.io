Business Development Manager - IT Sales- Cloud | Delhi/NCR

5-7 Years

Business Development Manager - IT Sales- Cloud

Job Description

  • Acquire an in-depth understanding of the Corporate segment within the Delhi/NCR to identify key clients and to promote and sell the entire range of IT datacentre related technologies.
  • Establish relationships with customers and secure contracts and execute strategy for account and achieve assigned sales quotas and targets.
  • Drive the entire sales cycle customer engagement to closed sales.
  • Qualify opportunity inside account and create solutions.
  • Consult about business challenges and requirements, as well as the range of options and cost benefits of each.
  • Maintain a high level of relevant domain knowledge in order to have meaningful conversations with prospects.
  • Make presentations to senior managers and decision makers.
  • Draft and deliver proposals.
  • Work with technical staff and product specialists where required to address customer requirements.
  • Develop and maintain key account plans that identify opportunities for company to deliver value, strategic motivators, main stakeholders, buying processes and forecasted sales.
  • Report on sales activity.
  • Keep detailed notes on prospect and customer interactions.
  • Provide forecasts on best case and most likely sales volumes over relevant time periods.
  • Work with delivery teams to proactively address problems.

Skills & Qualifications:

  • Prior experience of selling Cloud services like AWS, Azure. Minimum 3-4 years.
  • Certified Cloud Sales professional.
  • Experience of Advice, assessment, and migration of workload from Onprem to cloud infra. Should be able to understand hybrid cloud environment.
  • Experience of selling IaaS, PaaS should be aware of the benefit of server less, IAC, SAP Hana on Cloud.
  • Involved in the business case for migration of onprem/private cloud infra to cloud environment like Aws, Azure. Understand Lift and shift or Lift and transform approach.
  • Working Knowledge and awareness of the use case of cloud infra, PaaS and understand the concept of server less, infrastructure as code, SAP Hana on cloud etc is highly desirable.
  • Knowledge of regulatory frameworks like IRDAI, PCI-DSS, RBI and ISO-27001 are desirable for understanding the cloud feasibility and BI on the businesses.
  • Relationship with Public cloud OEM will be an added advantage like AWS, MS-Azure, VMC, IBM Cloud or Google Cloud etc.
  • Working relations with Distributors like Redington, TechData etc.
