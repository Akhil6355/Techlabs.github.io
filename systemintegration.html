Solution

For new or existing businesses, Tech9labs utilizes its engagement process (strategy, plan, design, deploy, operate, and review) to install your new IT system and support it long-term. Tech9labs uses its expertise to combat the challenges of integration and inter-operability. We are passionate about delivering the best technology to achieve outstanding results while flawless integration.

Approach

The key to a successful outcome is sound approach. Working closely with our client, deeply understand the requirements and describe the best suited business model, map the competitive landscape, define the value position, and design the right solution to deliver the best technology to achieve outstanding results.

Plan/Strategy

We explore, listen, dig, uncover. We get to know the customer and business needs. How do you improve the ROI and achieve best TCO? What are the key metrics to focus on? From this understanding we plan projects so is carefully thought-out, well organized, and effectively executed.

We value and respect the aspiration of every employee in the organization and believe that the growth of the organization is interdependent with the growth of the employee. We have imbibed some of the best practices to ensure that an individual with the right attitude, competence and potential are on the fast-growth track.

At Tech9labs, our main assets are our people, our reputation, our image and our commitment to excellence! We make working an experience in itself by providing the right balance of personal growth and organizational growth.

Design

We work closely with our clients to produce the optimal and interoperable solution design. Our approach is always to design the best-in-class solutions with the optimal resources. Our goal is to achieve your business objectives.

Deploy

Once the optimal solution is designed and detailed scope is discussed, we quickly deploy within stipulated time committed.

Operate

We always want to be associated as long-term technology partner. We stay engaged after deployment to ensure project success, identify gaps and further solutions, and to help you meet the desired objectives and reach even greater heights.

Review

We iterate and recommend continuous improvements to help you maximize your ROI/TCO. We offer technologies and products from IBM, HP, NetApp, EMC2, VMware, Microsoft, Cisco, Juniper, Citrix, Symantec, Oracle, Red Hat, Schneider, and APC.
